🌿 Adhyāya II – Vṛndāvana-cintā: The Inner Vision of the Dhāma

An inward pilgrimage—seeing Vṛndāvana as living consciousness: its saints, companions, moods, and the heart as temple.

Section 1
I. Saints, Sādhus, and the Eternal Servants

Those who carry Vṛndāvana within; the lineages, vows, and sevā that keep the lamp burning.

Section 2
II. The Living Vṛndāvana

The dhāma as organism—trees, river, breeze, cattle, dust—all responding to Kṛṣṇa’s flute.

Section 3
III. The Sakhīs and Their Bhāvas

Companions in intimate service—their rasas, confidences, and the art of sacred friendship.

IV. Vṛndāvana as the Heart’s Inner World
